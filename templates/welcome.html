It's Boggle Time

Rules

  • Game lasts for 60 seconds.
  • Search for words and enter as many unique words as you can.
  • To form a word connect adjacent letters.
  • Adjacency can be orthagonal and/or diagonal.
  • You may change direction as many times as you want.
  • Letter instances may only be used once per word.
  • Words may be one or more letters long.
  • Scored words are worth 1 point per letter.
